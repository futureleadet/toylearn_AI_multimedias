## 화면 크기 생성 비율

- 1:1 = 1024 : 1024
- 15:8 = 1024 : 546
```
15/8=1024/x

To solve for x, multiply 15 by x and 8 by 1024, and then set the two products equal to each other.

15∗x=8∗1024
15x=8192

To isolate x, divide both sides of the equation by 15.

x=8192/15
x=546.1333...

The value of x is approximately 546.13.
```
